## Interview Structure

### 1. HOOK (2 min)
> Teaser moment - most emotional/surprising clip from interview

### 2. INTRO (3 min)
- Host welcome
- Guest introduction
- Why this athlete, why now

### 3. ORIGIN STORY (10 min)
- How did you start in [sport]?
- First memory of competition?
- Family support/sacrifice?

### 4. THE GRIND (10 min)
- Training routine
- Lowest moment in career
- What almost made you quit?

### 5. MENTAL GAME (8 min)
- How do you handle pressure?
- Mental health journey (if comfortable)
- Pre-competition rituals

### 6. ROAD TO MILAN-CORTINA (8 min)
- Qualification journey
- What does this Olympics mean to you?
- Goals for the Games

### 7. BEYOND THE SPORT (5 min)
- Life outside training
- Causes/passions
- Advice to young athletes

### 8. CLOSE (2 min)
- Where can people follow you?
- Final message
- Host outro
